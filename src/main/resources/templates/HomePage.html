About k-shop

k-shop connects university students looking to buy and sell secondhand goods. Whether it's textbooks, electronics, or furniture, we make it easy to find what you need from fellow students in your community.

Features

  • Browse secondhand books, furniture, electronics, and more
  • Connect with local students for easy pickups or deliveries
  • Safe and easy transactions with student-to-student interactions
